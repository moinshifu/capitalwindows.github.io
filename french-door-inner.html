ALUMINIUM DOORS

French Door

Overview

SECURITY - All of our French Doors come with double glazing as standard, and can easily have custom security locks built in, as well as changeable hardware to suit your taste and style. French Doors are tough and durable, meaning they are a great safe option.

DESIGN – Our range of French Doors come in range of materials, colours and finishes. You can choose from uPVC, Timber or Aluminium, making them adaptable to your home décor and personal taste.

LOW MAINTENANCE - If you opt for uPVC or Aluminium French Doors, they are easy to maintain with a quick wipe down once every while. Timber usually requires more maintenance as it needs varnishing to maintain its look.

ADAPTABLE - French Doors are hinged doors which often come in pairs and swing outwards. They suit both traditional and modern households due to the range of materials and finishes they come in.

SPACE - French Doors create space and let in the natural light, giving you a more open feel and keeping it cool in the summer months.
